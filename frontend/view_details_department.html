Department Overview

The Oncology Department is dedicated to the diagnosis, treatment, and care of patients with cancer. The department includes specialists such as medical oncologists, surgical oncologists, and radiation experts who work together to provide complete cancer care.

The Cardiology Department provides complete heart care services including diagnosis, emergency treatment, heart monitoring, and long-term treatment plans for patients with cardiac conditions.

The General Department provides primary healthcare services for patients. Doctors diagnose common illnesses, prescribe medicines, perform routine checkups, and guide patients toward specialized treatment if needed.
